# PassReady Prep — Claude Code Instructions

## Product identity
PassReady Prep is **NCMHCE exam preparation** — clinical case simulations, flashcards,
diagnostic references, a readiness predictor, and a pass guarantee. It is a **study aid**.
It is **not counseling services**, **not continuing education (CE)**, and **not approved by
any licensing or certification board.**

---

## Continuing-education credentials & board marks — READ BEFORE WRITING ANY MARKETING OR CONTENT COPY

This rule keeps getting violated across sessions. It is documented here permanently.

**Corporate structure:**
- **GA Integrated Therapeutic Perspectives LLC (GAITP)** is a **counseling practice**.
- **CounselorReady (CR)** is the **subsidiary that handles education.** CR holds the
  continuing-education provider approvals — **NBCC (ACEP #7760), plus state board and social
  work board approvals** — and those approvals apply to **CR's CE courses.**
- The approvals are registered under GAITP's legal name because boards register by legal
  entity. That does **not** make GAITP-as-a-whole, or PassReady, a CE provider.

**On any PassReady surface (landing page, app, the NCMHCE study-guide ebook, its Amazon/KDP
listing, or any PassReady marketing):**
- **NEVER** display any specific board's CE-provider mark, approval number, approval
  statement, or logo — **NBCC / ACEP #7760, the state board's, the social work board's, or
  any other.** Those marks belong to CR's CE courses. Putting them on exam prep implies a
  board endorses the exam prep — none do — and violates the boards' own mark-use rules.
- Descriptive references to the exam itself are fine (e.g., "scored the way NBCC scores you,"
  "the current NCMHCE format"). Those are not marks or approval claims.
- If a soft credibility line is wanted, a **generic, unmarked descriptor is acceptable and
  honest.** CR genuinely holds CE-provider approvals from multiple boards, so
  *"built by a board-approved continuing education provider"* is a true umbrella claim.
  **No board name, no approval number, no logo.**

**The one place specific approvals belong on a PassReady surface** is the Provider Disclosure
on `public/policies.html`, which is written to (a) state PassReady awards no CE, (b) scope the
approvals to CounselorReady, and (c) disclaim board affiliation. Do not strip or alter it.

**Do not "improve" or re-add a credential elsewhere.** If a task didn't ask for a credential
line, don't invent one.
