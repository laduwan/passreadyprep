Common questions

How do I claim my free CE course after passing?
Email your unofficial NCMHCE score report to us within 14 days. We'll send you a coupon code for your choice of TMH 601 or Mandated Reporter. Then sign up for a free account at CounselorReady.com, apply your code, and enable CE deadline reminders in your dashboard. We'll email you at 60, 30, and 7 days so you complete the course before your 90-day window closes.
Can I get a refund instead of extended access if I fail?
The guarantee covers extended access, not a refund. We want to keep helping you pass — that's the point.
What if my subscription expired before I got my score?
If your subscription was active on your exam date and you submit within 14 days, you're covered even if it expired in between.
How long is the extension if I don't pass?
Equal to the length of your original subscription. If you had a 3-month plan, you get 3 more months.
Is there a limit on how many times I can use the fail guarantee?
The guarantee applies once per subscription. If you re-subscribe and don't pass again, you're eligible again.
What counts as a "completed case"?
A case where you answered all questions and saw the results screen. Starting and abandoning a case doesn't count.
What is CounselorReady?
CounselorReady is our board-approved continuing education platform for licensed counselors. Once you pass the NCMHCE and get licensed, you'll need CE credits — your free course gets you started.
