Pass Guarantee Terms

Effective June 30, 2026 · Applies to the Pass Guarantee tier only

The Pass Guarantee is not a money-back offer. It guarantees continued access plus a bonus benefit, so you are supported whether you pass or need another attempt.

What You Get

The Pass Guarantee tier provides full access to the PassReady Prep library with no repayment ever, subject to the verification check-in below. Your access does not expire while these terms are met.

Dual Outcome

  • If you pass the NCMHCE — your first continuing-education course on CounselorReady is on us (a complimentary CE course, such as TMH601 or Mandated Reporter), redeemable within 90 days of submitting your passing result, along with a CounselorReady account to use it.
  • If you do not pass — your full access continues at no additional cost so you can keep preparing for your next attempt. No repayment is required.

6-Month Candidate Verification Check-In

To keep the guarantee tied to a genuine, single candidate and prevent credential sharing, the Pass Guarantee includes a one-time verification check-in approximately six months after purchase. At that point we ask you to confirm one of the following:

  • your scheduled NCMHCE exam date, or
  • your exam result (a passing or non-passing outcome / score report).

This check-in is a quick confirmation, not a payment gate. Full access is restored promptly (typically within one business day) after you submit it. Accounts that do not complete the check-in may have access paused until they do.

Eligibility & Good-Faith Use

The guarantee assumes good-faith preparation: that you are an individual candidate using your own account, actively studying on the platform, and sitting for the NCMHCE in its current format. The guarantee does not cover account sharing, fraudulent submissions, or misuse, and does not guarantee a passing score — it guarantees the access and benefits described above.
